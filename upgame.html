Overview

What is Ugame? This is an online store that provides a selection of the best offers of computer games. You can easily pre-order, buy a game, or sign up for a big discount tracking.

The mission is to transform the world of online shopping. Daily purchases of various blockbusters can now be made as profitably and conveniently as possible.

My Role

My Role

As the sole product developer, I was involved in every aspect of the product development process, from brainstorming the next big product innovation to pixel tweaking just before launch.

In more detail, I led eight projects:

  • 1. Creation of an original Design system. This system is the main engine for standardization and harmonization of product design across devices.
  • 2. Creating a serious backend architecture capable of withstanding heavy loads.
  • 3. Organization of modularity of visual components and structuring of a specific file system.
  • 4. Creation of the front-end part, layout, animation of elements for a better user experience.
  • 5. Creation of marketing images in Photoshop, setting up Yandex Direct, Google Adwords, Facebook advertising platforms, competent preparation of an advertising plan and development of an information project
  • 6. Creation of an investment project for the development of the product.
  • 7. Attraction of investments, presentation of the project to investors.
  • 8. Registration of a legal entity and full automation of the business process.

The Problem

The Problem

  • 1. The UX study showed that young people had a problem using my competitors' products: they stopped seeing them as fashionable and beautiful. Colors, fonts and marketing techniques that have been used for 10 years now look stale against the backdrop of fashion trends.
  • 2. Prices for digital products of competitors directly depend on the financial burden of the company.
  • 3. The business aims to improve my brand design, moving from serious and sometimes boring styles to younger and more memorable ones.
  • 4. Realizing that "search" is a central part of the user's journey to finding the best deals, I needed to completely rethink and redesign this feature.
  • 5. The design of the main page and the search engine caused a redesign of all pages of the platform.
  • 6. Prices for digital products of competitors directly depend on the financial burden of the company.
