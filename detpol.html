Overview

The Murmansk children's polyclinic has been treating children under 18 for a very long time. The site is visited by people of different ages and in many cases these are elderly people who find it difficult to find information on the site.

The mission is to make it easy for all people to understand the interface, find the information they need and easily make an appointment with a doctor.
